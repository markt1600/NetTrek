NETTREK

Survive the galaxy. Capture planets. Get stronger.

The mission

You command one ship in hostile space. Three rival fleets are out to destroy you — and each other. Stay alive as long as you can.

Capture planets to grow stronger. Each planet you take adds to your maximum hull, shields, and energy — and friendly planets repair and refuel you when you orbit them. The AI does the same, so move fast.

Structural health (0–100%) is your ship's core integrity. Damage gets through to it whenever your shields are down or have failed. As structural health degrades, systems start failing:

  • <50% — torpedo bays offline
  • <25% — phasers offline
  • <10% — warp drive offline
  • 0% — game over

Final score = kills + captures + seconds survived.

Energy & combat

One Energy pool powers everything — warp drive, shields, phasers, and torpedoes. Shields up drains energy steadily and absorbs incoming fire (no hull damage); shields down lets you save energy and recharge faster, but a single torpedo will hurt. If shield HP hits zero, shields collapse and can't be raised for a couple seconds.

Torpedoes are limited (your magazine size depends on ship class) and reload one at a time over a few seconds — faster while orbiting a friendly repair planet. Torps gently home onto a locked target out to 5 screens, but accuracy drops with distance: 100% at 1 screen, 20% at 5 screens.

Enemy torps lock onto you the same way. Your radar will blink red and the HUD will show the torpedo's distance closing. You have 3 flares per life — press D to deploy one. Each flare has a 50% chance to divert the nearest incoming torpedo.

Targeting & firing

Left-click an enemy ship (within ~3000u) or press F to lock the nearest one. While locked, your ship auto-pivots toward the target and your speed is capped at combat speed (warp 4) so you can come around. If you're moving too fast you'll fly past — slow down to engage.

Your firing cone is roughly ±20° dead ahead. Phasers and torpedoes auto-fire when the target is inside the cone and in range. Watch for the green FIRE READY reticle.

Radar & scanners

The universe is huge — 100 screens square. Your radar shows the area centered on you in two ranges, toggle with R: SHORT (5 screens) shows ship class markers; LONG (20 screens) shows only contact dots. Click any ship on the radar to lock it and head toward it. Detail revealed depends on distance: in the same screen → full stats; short range → class & rough hull; long range → just "contact".

Orbit an enemy planet, press C, hold for 5 seconds with no enemy within range.
