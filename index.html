NETTREK

A galactic conquest game inspired by Netrek (1988) & NetTrek (1985)

Tip: click Instructions below to learn the controls before launching.

How to Play

Goal. Help your team capture every enemy planet. Each team starts with 10 planets; when only your team has planets standing, you win.

Movement & combat

  • Right-click on the tactical map: set course toward that point.
  • 0–9: set warp speed (your ship has a maximum).
  • Left-click on enemy ship: fire phasers (limited range, instant hit, costs fuel).
  • T: fire torpedo toward your last clicked point (or current heading).
  • Y: tractor beam lock on hovered target; U: pressor (push) lock; I: release beams.
  • S: shields up/down (down lets you refit and beam armies, but you take more damage).

Planets, armies & capture

  • O: orbit nearest planet (must be slow & close).
  • B: bomb enemy planet you're orbiting (kills armies).
  • Z: beam armies down to a planet (drop to capture / reinforce).
  • X: beam armies up from a friendly planet (shields must be DOWN).
  • You can only carry up to (kills × 2) armies. Get kills first!
  • Drop armies onto an enemy planet whose army count is 0 to capture it.

Refit & ranks

  • R: refit to a new ship class when orbiting a friendly planet with shields down.
  • Ships: Scout, Destroyer, Cruiser, Battleship, Assault. Each trades speed, firepower, armies.
  • Repair planets (wrench) speed hull/shield repair. Fuel depots (●) recharge fuel faster.

Display

  • Left: tactical view centered on your ship.
  • Right: galactic map — click to set destination, see all planets.
  • Bottom: HUD with stats and a message log.
